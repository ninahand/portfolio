agency_does_not_exist
A website exploring the holes in the public’s access to information about the US federal government’s massive budget, paired with a printed translation in the form of a fold-out zine. Coded using the US Federal Spending API and tone.js. Zine/poster printed on vellum and bound together with metal jump rings.
